Located on the crest of First Mountain on the Montclair/Verona border. Claridge House offers the perfect lifestyle. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline to the east and Watchung Mountain to the west.

Minutes from upscale shopping and dining, movie and theatre venues, Verona Park, the world class Montclair Art Museum, and so much more. Commute to Manhattan by train or bus.

Spacious, balconied residences, studios to four-bedroom duplex penthouses. All feature generously large rooms, window walls, and ample storage.
